# Design Brief — Revicar

## Niche
Auto repair / mechanic shop — multi-brand, full-service (mechanics, bodywork & paint, electrical, AC, tyres)

## Mood & Vibe
Trustworthy local mechanic meets modern web design. Warm and approachable — the "your neighbourhood mechanic" feel where you know the guys by name — but presented with a level of polish and visual impact that no local competitor has. Professional without being corporate.

## Colour Direction
- **Primary**: Deep blue (trust, reliability — from their logo)
- **Secondary**: White / light gray (clean, readable)
- **Background**: Mix of dark sections (hero, dramatic moments) and light sections (services, contact)
- **Accents**: Red (from logo — used sparingly for CTAs, highlights, hover states)

## References
- [stationsdr.pt](https://www.stationsdr.pt) — dark hero with video, red accents on white, icon-based service grid, bold typography, checklist trust signals. Take the structure and professionalism but make it warmer, more local, and more visually impressive with animations.

## Hero Section
**Go big — this should impress.** Dark, cinematic hero with strong visual impact:
- 3D or scroll-triggered animation (e.g. car parts assembling, engine components floating, tools in motion)
- Video background as fallback if 3D is too heavy
- Parallax depth effects on scroll
- Bold headline with animated text reveal
- Trust signals below hero (4.5★ rating, 124+ reviews, years in business)
- Primary CTA: "Pedir Orçamento" (Get a Quote)

The hero should make someone think "this is NOT the usual mechanic website."

## Key Differentiators to Emphasize
1. **Full-service, all brands** — show the breadth visually (icon grid or animated service cards: mechanics, bodywork, electrical, AC, tyres). Not just oil changes — they handle everything.
2. **Trusted local shop** — 4.5★ with 124 reviews, personal service, customers come back. Show real reviews, rating prominently, maybe years of experience.
3. **Convenience** — one stop for everything your car needs. Easy to contact, easy to book, straightforward process.

## Things to Avoid
- Generic corporate/franchise look — this is a local family shop, not a chain
- Stock photos of random mechanics — use AI-generated images that feel authentic to a Portuguese workshop
- Cluttered layouts — keep it clean and impactful
- Discount/cheap vibes — position as quality and trustworthy, not budget

## Section Notes
- **Services section**: Icon grid with the 5 service areas (Mecânica, Chapa e Pintura, Eletricista, Climatização, Pneus). Each could have a subtle hover animation or expand on click.
- **About section**: Story of the local shop, years serving Carcavelos. Split layout with workshop image.
- **Reviews section**: Real Google reviews, emphasize the 4.5★ rating. Carousel or staggered cards.
- **Contact section**: Map embed, phone, address, hours. Simple and functional.
- **FAQ section**: Standard accordion — common questions about services, pricing, turnaround.
- **Gallery strip**: Workshop photos, team, cars being worked on.
- **Scroll animations throughout**: Sections should reveal with motion as user scrolls — fade-ins, slide-ups, parallax. The whole page should feel alive.
